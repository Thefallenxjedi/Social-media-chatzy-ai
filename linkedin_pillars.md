# Chatzy AI LinkedIn Content Strategy (The 4 Pillars)

This document outlines the strategic split for LinkedIn content between the Company Page and Personal Pages. 

## 1. Company Page: Product & Feature Announcements
**Goal:** Build platform authority, showcase momentum, and announce new capabilities.
**Formats:**
- Video announcements (Founder and Team-led).
- Feature updates (UI mockups, Carousels, Bento Box images).
- Latest trends in the AI world (Positioning Chatzy within the broader market).

## 2. Company Page: Education & Case Studies
**Goal:** Prove ROI and educate the market on how to use AI agents effectively.
**Formats:**
- Customer Case Studies (Showcasing real customer stories, metrics, and outcomes).
- Educational posts (How-to guides, automation best practices).

## 3. Personal Page: Storytelling & Problem Statements (Lead Gen)
**Goal:** Generate high-intent inbound leads by relating to founder/operator struggles.
**Formats:**
- Lead posts: Narrative-driven stories about Chatzy's journey or a specific customer problem.
- Contrarian takes on customer support, sales, and automation.
- Focus heavily on the "Why" and the business outcome (e.g., saving time, generating revenue while sleeping).

## 4. Personal Page: Feature Deep-Dives (Lead Gen)
**Goal:** Highlight specific Chatzy AI capabilities to attract users looking for specific solutions.
**Formats:**
- Highlighting how a specific feature (e.g., Omnichannel inbox, WhatsApp calling, Multi-agent routing) solves a massive headache.
- Short, punchy posts that end with a direct CTA to try the feature.
- Leverage the `features.md` document for exact capabilities and limits to discuss.
