## 2. LinkedIn Post Copy
If you run a Shopify store, you’re likely losing 70% of your sales to abandoned carts.

To fix it, you probably set up the standard 3-part recovery email sequence.

Here is the cold truth: 
Standard e-commerce email open rates hover around 15%. Click rates are below 2%. 

In plain terms, you are sending discount codes to an inbox your customer checks once a week. 

If they do open it, it’s usually hours or days later. By then, the buying intent is gone. They’ve already closed the tab, moved on, or bought from a competitor.

But what if you could nudge them where they actually spend their time?

WhatsApp open rates are 98%. 
90% of messages are read within 3 minutes.

When we helped an e-commerce brand migrate their recovery from email to automated WhatsApp journeys on Chatzy AI, they saw:
* Messages sent automatically 15 minutes after checkout is abandoned.
* Customer replies routed instantly to the support inbox if they have questions.
* A 4x increase in recovered revenue in week one.

Email isn't dead for everything. But for capturing high-intent cart abandoners, it's a leaky bucket.

Operators don't wait for emails to get read. They go where the conversation is happening.
