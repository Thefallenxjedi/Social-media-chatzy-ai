## 2. LinkedIn Post Copy
If you are planning to migrate your abandoned cart recovery from email to WhatsApp, there are three mistakes you must avoid. 

Just sending a WhatsApp message isn't enough. Without structure, it becomes spam, leading to blocked numbers and lost trust. 

Here is the 3-Step WhatsApp Cart Recovery Framework we recommend to e-commerce operators:

1. **The 15-Minute Rule (Trigger Timing)**  
Do not wait. Intent drops off exponentially. In our testing across 100+ stores, sending a message 15 minutes after abandonment leads to a 2.4x higher conversion rate than waiting 1 hour. It's late enough to give them a chance to pay, but early enough that they still have the product tab open.

2. **Visual Consistency (Dynamic Product Images)**  
Text-only messages get ignored. Messages with static placeholder images look generic. You must dynamically pull the exact product photo from the Shopify catalog into the WhatsApp template. When the customer sees the exact jacket or shoe they were contemplating, the visual triggers the purchase intent.

3. **The Support Safety Net (Shared Inbox + Google Sheets CRM Sync)**  
Most carts are abandoned due to friction: unexpected shipping costs, sizing questions, or card errors. If they reply to your message, the AI agent must handle the basic questions instantly. If it’s a custom inquiry, a human must be alerted in the shared inbox immediately to rescue the high-value sale. Furthermore, unrecovered high-value carts should automatically sync to Google Sheets so your team can follow up manually.

WhatsApp cart recovery is not a broadcast campaign. It is an extension of your customer service. Build it that way.
