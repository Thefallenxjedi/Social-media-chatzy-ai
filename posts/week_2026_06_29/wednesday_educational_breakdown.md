## 2. LinkedIn Post Copy
How do you build a WhatsApp abandoned cart recovery flow that actually converts? 

It’s not about blasting users with spam. It’s about building a structured, conversational journey. 

Here is the exact step-by-step framework we set up inside the Chatzy AI workflow builder:

1. **The Trigger: Shopify Abandoned Checkout**  
When a user inputs their contact details on Shopify but exits before paying, the checkout event fires. This triggers the Chatzy journey.

2. **The Delay: 15 Minutes**  
Do not message them immediately—it feels intrusive. Do not wait 24 hours—intent will be gone. We found 15 minutes is the sweet spot. 

3. **The Message: Dynamic Template**  
We send an official Meta-approved WhatsApp template message. We inject the customer’s name, the exact item name, and the product image URL dynamically. This isn’t a generic "Hey! Buy this." It’s a personalized visual reminder of what they wanted.

4. **The Fork: AI vs Human**  
If the user replies, we fork the journey:
* Simple questions (pricing, sizing, returns) are handled instantly by the Conversational AI agent trained on the store's knowledge base.
* Custom/Complex questions route immediately to the Shared Omnichannel Inbox, notifying a human support agent to step in.

5. **The Safety Net: Google Sheets Sync**  
If the cart value is high (e.g. >$200) and they don’t purchase within 2 hours, we trigger an API action to sync their details to a Google Sheet. This alerts the sales team to follow up manually.

Building a workflow like this doesn't require a developer. With our visual editor, it takes about 10 minutes to deploy.
