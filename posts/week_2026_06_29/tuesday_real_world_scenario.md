## 2. LinkedIn Post Copy
It’s 10:30 PM on a Tuesday.

Sarah lands on a Shopify store, adds a $120 winter jacket to her cart, enters her details, and then... gets distracted. Her phone rings, or the baby wakes up. She closes her browser. 

In a standard setup, here is what happens:
Nothing. Or maybe an email gets queued to go out 24 hours later. By Wednesday night, Sarah has already forgotten about the jacket, or bought it elsewhere. 

Here is what happens with Chatzy AI:

* **10:45 PM (15 minutes later):** Sarah gets a WhatsApp message. It’s personalized. It has a high-quality photo of the exact green jacket she was looking at. No aggressive sales pitch. Just a friendly message: *"Hi Sarah, we saved your cart! Do you have any questions about the sizing or material before you order?"*
* **10:47 PM:** Sarah replies: *"Does this run true to size or should I size up?"*
* **10:47 PM:** The AI agent, trained on the store’s knowledge base, replies instantly: *"It runs slightly large, Sarah. Most customers recommend sizing down. Since you added a Medium, a Small might fit you better."*
* **10:48 PM:** Sarah replies: *"Perfect, can you change it to Small?"*

The AI update is made, and a checkout link is generated right inside the chat. Sarah clicks, pays via Apple Pay, and the sale is completed. 

Total time from cart abandonment to purchase: 18 minutes. 
Zero human intervention required. 

This isn't a future concept. It’s what modern e-commerce stores are doing right now to recover checkout sales while they sleep.

If you aren't conversational at the point of intent, you are losing sales to someone who is.
