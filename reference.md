## Key Takeaways for Chatzy AI Content Strategy
* **Show, Don't Tell:** Use videos, screenshots, and visual walkthroughs heavily to demonstrate the product in action. Visuals are a primary driver of engagement.
* **Focus on Real Users:** Highlight specific customers, their distinct business challenges, and the exact operational impact of the AI agent. Use direct quotes from founders and operators.
* **Keep It Conversational:** The tone should be direct, operator-focused, and free of excessive marketing jargon or hype.
* **Clear Formatting:** Use short paragraphs, bullet points, and strong hooks (e.g., "Most brands track deflection. That's the wrong metric.").
* **Actionable Call to Actions (CTAs):** Often drive engagement via comments (e.g., "Comment 'VOICE' for a free month") or direct them to an insightful full-length case study link.
